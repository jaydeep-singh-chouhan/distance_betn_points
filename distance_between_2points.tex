\textbf{\textsl{1. Find the distance between the following pairs of points :}}

\vspace{1cm}
(iii) (66,25),(99,69)\\

  Answer- $$ x_1=66 ,x_2=99 ,y_1=25,y_2=69$$\\
$$ d=\sqrt{((x_2-x_1)^2+(y_2-y_1)^2)}$$
\vspace{0.2cm}
$$ d=\sqrt{((99-66)^2 + (69-25)^2)}=\sqrt{(33^2 + 44^2)}$$\\
$$ d= 55$$
